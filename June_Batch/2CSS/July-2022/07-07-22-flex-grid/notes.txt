How to use Font-awesome icon? (third party plugins)
Step1:https://fontawesome.com/v4/icons/
step2:Please download the folder from website
https://fontawesome.com/v4/get-started/
step3:Please unzip and keep in particular location
step4:rename the folder font-awesome
step5:remove one nested folder of font-awesome 
step6:please add the link tag in your html:
 <link rel="stylesheet" href="./font-awesome/css/font-awesome.min.css">
step7:
